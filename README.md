# AI SEO & Search Visibility Research Hub (2026)

Hey there! I put this repository together to serve as my personal library for real, unfiltered search data. 

With standard search engines shifting heavily into AI Overviews, ChatGPT answers, SEO and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) this year, the old playbook for ranking websites is mostly broken. I needed a single, clean place to store raw notes, podcast audio transcripts, and breakdowns away from the noise of social media algorithms.

### 📂 What's Inside This Hub
* `/research/sources.md` - My master directory tracking every operator, their platform links, and a quick breakdown of their strategies.
* `/research/linkedin-posts/` - Hard-to-find text case studies stripped of platform tracking codes and formatted for quick reading.
* `/research/youtube-transcripts/` - Complete text transcripts of strategy breakdowns, news roundups, and over-the-shoulder tutorials.
* `/research/other/` - Deep-dive newsletter issues, substack podcast files, and platform documentation.
