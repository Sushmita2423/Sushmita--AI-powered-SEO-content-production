## Why did we create the Entrepreneurship Uncensored Podcast?

In the coming weeks, James Dooley and I will launch a [new podcast](https://www.youtube.com/@EntrepreneurshipUncensoredPod). Both of us have a lot of content out there and have our own individual podcasts, so why launch this new one?

We have long wanted to collaborate. Although Dooley is slightly older, we are close in age and share similar interests and business outlooks. However, we often have disagreements and assist each other in both SEO and business. Having a sounding board is beneficial because it allows us to receive others’ opinions and gain an outside perspective. We both believe that expanding our [discussion topics](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt39186452/) is necessary, and given our extensive business and life experiences, we believe a broader podcast would enable us to enjoy ourselves while learning from others, family members, and individuals from different industries, without fear of offending anyone or upsetting people in our industry. We are both past the stage of caring about what others think.

## What guests will you be able to get on the Entrepreneurship Uncensored Podcast?

We want to have as many different guests on the podcast as possible. We want to talk to people from other fields, like business, entrepreneurs’ friends, and family, not just SEO experts. [Subscribe to the YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@EntrepreneurshipUncensoredPod) to see who we get on; I promise there will be exciting guests and insightful conversations that will provide a lot of value for other entrepreneurs.

## Who should listen to the Entrepreneurship Uncensored Podcast?

The podcast’s stories and experiences will help anyone interested in business, not just digital marketers. Although James Dooley and I have a background in digital marketing and will feature guests from that industry, our goal is to include a diverse range of entrepreneurs on the [podcast](https://jamesdooley.com/podcasts/entrepreneurship-uncensored-podcast/) to share their stories and experiences, from which we can also learn.

## Who Should Not Listen to the Entrepreneurship Uncensored Podcast?

We would never say it’s not for anyone, but James and I are definitely not ones to fluff things up; hard work, dedication and taking action without all the usual BS is our things, and we aim to make sure that the tone and the guests we have on have no filters on, as we are sick and tired of hearing all the usual BS out there, so the aim is to be as positive and direct as we can with the tone and try and get to the bottom of how entrepreneurs get success.
